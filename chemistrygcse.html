Chemistry GCSE

GCSE Chemistry is the study of the make-up, behaviour and properties of different matter, and of the elements of our Earth and within its atmosphere. On this page you will find links to useful notes, where to find past papers and practice materials, and some handy-dandy videos to help you through this complex science.
